About Us

Complex Ventures is a digital strategy agency based in Brooklyn’s DUMBO neighborhood. We focus on brands and organizations with a purpose — brands that are committed to advocacy, sustainability, and other mission-driven initiatives in addition to their core line of business.


With our experience in digital strategy, web design and development, content creation, social media optimization, and advertising, we create and implement results-focused online communications campaigns that help our clients gain attention, users, and customers. We understand the unique challenges purpose-driven companies face and take an intuitive approach to every stage of planning and execution while keeping our clients’ core values at top of mind.
